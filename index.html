FAQs

Where Should i Place My Indoor Plants ?
Indoor plants should be placed where they receive the appropriate light level for species, commonly near windows but away from direct harsh sunlight.
How can i make my indoor plants grow fast ?
Fertilizing them regularly with appropriate nutrients and maintaining adequate light and water helps plants grow faster.
How can i keep my plants bug free ?
Keeping plants clean, quarantining new plants, and regular inspection with insecticidal soap or neem oil helps keep bugs away.
How often should i water my plants ?
Water needs vary by species. Generally, water when topsoil feels dry; avoid overwatering to prevent root rot.
Do indoor plants purified air ?
Certain indoor plants can improve air quality by removing toxins and releasing oxygen, promoting a healthier indoor environment.
